How It Works: Precision AI Detection

01. Scan

The app silently scans for suspicious Wi-Fi and Bluetooth signals.

02. Score (AI)

An *on-device AI model* instantly scores the risk of each signal.

03. Alert

If a threat is found, the phone vibrates, and the app shows a discreet alert.

04. Empower

The app explains exactly why something is suspicious, empowering you to stay safe.
